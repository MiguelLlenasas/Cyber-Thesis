Password Vulnerability Classification System

PRIVACY AND CONSENT NOTICE

Before using the Password Vulnerability Classification System, please read the following information carefully.

Privacy and Data Protection

Your privacy is important to us.

The password you enter is used only to analyze its characteristics and determine its possible vulnerability to different password attack methods.

The system does not permanently store, save, or share your actual password. Instead, it analyzes only password features such as its length, character types, and patterns to generate the classification result.

Any information collected during this research will remain confidential and will be used only for academic and research purposes.

Research Participation

This system was developed as part of a 2026 research project at the University of Cabuyao.

By clicking "Accept" button you voluntarily agree to participate in the evaluation of this research system. Your participation helps the researchers evaluate and improve the accuracy of the password vulnerability classification model.

Your participation is completely voluntary, and you may stop using the system at any time.

Understanding the Results

The classification and recommendations provided by this system are intended for educational and research purposes only.

The results are based on the password's structural characteristics and common password attack patterns. They should be used as a guide to help improve password creation and should not be considered a guarantee that a password is completely secure or impossible to crack.
