Data Privacy

We prioritize the anonymity of user-generated content. All passwords submitted for testing are processed locally and compared against trained models. No plain-text data is permanently stored on our servers.

Research Integrity

This system is part of a 2026 Cybersecurity Thesis Project. By clicking accept, you contribute to the validation of our supervised machine learning model designed to identify common attack vectors.

Liability

The "Crack Tester" provides vulnerability estimations based on pattern recognition. It should be used as a guideline, not an absolute guarantee of cryptographic strength.
